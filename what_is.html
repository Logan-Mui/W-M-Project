What is Misinformation?

Introduction

Misinformation defined by the oxford dictionary as wrong or misleading information.

In essence, anything that is written, said, or percieved by someone else that is false can be defined as misinformation

Misinformation can be represented on the internet in various ways such as:

Fake News:

Fake news is fabrcated news with the intent of misleading or confusing readers

Misleading News

News Stories that use factual information out of context or in a misleading way

Satire

Satire is fake or ironic news intended for the sake of entertainment
